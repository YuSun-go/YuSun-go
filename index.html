I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University advised by Professor Boyuan Chen. I am interested in Robot Data-Driven Control in Dynamic Environments, Robotic Representation Learning, Multi-modality Learning, and Computer Vision. My research aims to help robots independently explore and understand the world, so that in the future they will become not only smarter assistants but also trustworthy friends 🤖.

I obtained the Master's degree in Electrical Engineering at Columbia University (2022) and the Bachelor's degree of Electrical and Automation Engineering from Nanjing Normal University (2020). I was also a short-term student at Rice University majoring in Electrical Engineering (2021).

I was a research assistant in Robot Learning and Control Group of Nanjing University for Professor Chunlin Chen and the leader of UAV Communication Link Denoising Group in Jiangsu Electromagnetic Compatibility Engineering with Professor Wei Yan. During my Master' study, I was working with Professor Shuran Song in Columbia Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Lab, and interned as a deep learning researcher at Kodiak Robotics.
